VISAYAS, CEBU CITY

Luzon, the northern region, is known for its savory dishes influenced by Spanish cuisine, such as Adobo and hearty stews. The Visayas, located in the central islands, showcases flavorful roasts and soups that highlight local creativity. In the south, Mindanao features bold spices and coconut-based recipes, reflecting its diverse cultural influences. Together, these regions create a rich tapestry of flavors deeply rooted in history and tradition.
